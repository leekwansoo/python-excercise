As we advance into the era of **Artificial Intelligence (AI) in healthcare**, medical laboratories are at the forefront of this transformation. AI applications in laboratory medicine include automated pattern recognition in diagnostic imaging, predictive analytics for patient outcomes, machine learning-powered quality control systems, and intelligent decision support for result interpretation. **Python serves as the primary programming language** powering these AI innovations, from developing neural networks for automated cell counting to creating predictive models for early disease detection.

- **Report Generation**: Create professional, standardized reports
- **Integration**: Connect different laboratory systems and databases
