Step 0: Download and Install the Vs_Code¶

from your browser access "https://code.visualstudio.com/download"

click the Download Button (Largest Top Button for window)

Goto download folder and click the "VSCodeUserSetup-x64-1.104.2.exe"

Install the vs code in the DeskTop(바탕화면)

move mouse to Desktop Vscode icon and click the right side button of your mouse then click the "추가옵션보기" => "Code로 열기" in the pop up menu
